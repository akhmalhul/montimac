NO. 1 SEWING SOLUTION SINCE 1931

High Speed, Increase Production, Less Waste Fabric.

Automation in the Stitching process of traditional and technical fabrics.

Monti-Mac has been specializing in innovative 100% MADE IN ITALY solutions for process automation in the textile industry since 1931.

Thanks to 90 years of experience in INDUSTRIAL AUTOMATION and the countless resources invested in RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT, MONTI MAC has the highest world quality standards for SEWING, cutting, handling and fabric handling solutions (traditional and technical) specializing in:

- PREPARATION of fabrics for finishing with head-to-tail stitching and automatic roll handling

- PREPARATION of selvedges for digital printing with patented cutting system managed by digital cameras

- SEWING, CUTTING and HANDLING geotextiles, TNT, Kevlar for the world of construction, defense, marine and landscaping.

MONTI MAC is based in COMO (IT) hub of fashion and fashion design but boasts over 40 points of sale / assistance centers in the world that guarantee a DYNAMIC POST SALE SERVICE.

A concentrate of RELIABILITY, FLEXIBILITY and ECO-SUSTAINABLE QUALITY at the service of over 3000 customers who have trusted our brand to date.
